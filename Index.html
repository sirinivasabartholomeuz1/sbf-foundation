Our Origin & Mission

The Sirinivasa Bartholomeuz Foundation (SBF) was founded by the loving wife and three sons of Mr. Bartholomeuz. Our core mission is to bring structured, sustainable aid to underprivileged individuals by focusing on direct, high-impact societal interventions.

"A life guided unconditionally by kindness, empathy, and quiet structural support for those in need."

We work collaboratively alongside verified NGOs, community leaders, and local government bodies to ensure transparency and accountability at every step of our delivery process.

The Four Pillars

Our structural efforts are categorized into four critical areas of development to systematically combat poverty and enable long-term societal resilience.

Poverty Alleviation

Providing direct financial aid, nutrition security packages, and structural shelter upgrades for families facing extreme systemic poverty.

Education & Youth

Empowering children through dedicated academic scholarships, distribution of learning materials, and modern digital literacy facilities.

Healthcare Support

Sponsoring local community health camps, essential medical supply logistics, and financial assistance for complex treatments.

Community Well-being

Developing specialized programs for marginalized demographics, youth mentorship initiatives, and localized environmental stewardship.
